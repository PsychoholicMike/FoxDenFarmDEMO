Fox Den Farm, Lyndeborough, NH

Welcome to my Fox Den Farm home page! I'm Amy Trudeau. I've been farming since 2009, and have owned Fox Den Farm since 2012. I went in to organic agriculture because I wanted to spend my life doing something that would have a positive impact on my community and the world. Here at Fox Den, I grow a wide variety of healthy, top-quality vegetables, following strict organic farming practices. I never use glyphosate (Roundup® and similar products) or any other herbicides or pesticides on my crops, and all of my fertilizers and soil amendments are OMRI approved. As often as possible, I source non-GMO certified organic seeds from Johnny's Selected Seeds in Maine. I use organic potting soil from the Vermont Compost Company®, and fish-based organic fertilizer from Neptune's Harvest in Massachusettes.
